## Patterns & Triggers
The complaints often arise when users are trying to:
* Build and maintain SaaS products
* Attract customers and generate revenue
* Compete with AI-generated SaaS apps
* Manage data using spreadsheets
* Develop software using better tools
* Scale their SaaS products
Common triggers include:
* Limited resources and funding
* Dependence on spreadsheets for data management
* Difficulty in finding useful advice or tools

## Objections & Blockers
Users often try to solve these problems by:
* Seeking advice from others
* Using existing tools and software
* Depending on their network and connections
* Investing time and money in their SaaS products
However, these attempts often fail due to:
* Limited resources and funding
* Lack of useful advice or tools
* Dependence on spreadsheets for data management
* Difficulty in competing with AI-generated SaaS apps

The biggest opportunity is to create a **SaaS Builder Tool** that simplifies the process of building and maintaining SaaS products, addressing the frequent and severe pain point of "I built and run a saas product solo (> $500k/year).
