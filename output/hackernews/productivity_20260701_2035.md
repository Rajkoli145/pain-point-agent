## Patterns & Triggers
People experience these complaints when:
* Trying to manage their time and prioritize tasks
* Using AI-powered tools to increase productivity
* Dealing with information overload and tool fatigue
* Attempting to measure and track productivity
* Working long hours and experiencing burnout
* Using spreadsheets for complex calculations and data management

## Objections & Blockers
People are stopped from solving these problems by:
* Lack of effective tools and solutions
* Difficulty in forming a mental model of integrated tools
* Inability to measure and track productivity accurately
* Limited awareness of available solutions and tools
* High expectations and pressure to be constantly productive
