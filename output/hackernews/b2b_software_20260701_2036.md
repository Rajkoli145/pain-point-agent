## Patterns & Triggers
The pain points mentioned in the posts are often triggered by situations such as:
* Trying to sell B2B software to large companies
* Dealing with high overhead costs for B2B SaaS companies
* Building and maintaining internal tools
* Managing data and spreadsheets
* Dealing with the disruption caused by AI and automation
* Trying to find and acquire customers
* Going through long sales cycles and experiencing low conversion rates

## Objections & Blockers
The objections and blockers mentioned in the posts include:
* Difficulty in convincing large companies to use their product
* High overhead costs for B2B SaaS companies
* Lack of expertise in tech sales
* Difficulty in building and maintaining internal tools
* Limited budget for software solutions
* Uncertainty about the effectiveness of AI and automation
