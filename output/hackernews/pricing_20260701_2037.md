## Patterns & Triggers
People experience these pain points when:
* Trying to adopt AI tools for their business
* Evaluating the cost-effectiveness of AI services
* Comparing pricing models of different AI providers
* Attempting to measure the ROI of AI investments
* Dealing with the proliferation of low-quality internal tools
* Struggling to understand the costs associated with AI services

## Objections & Blockers
People are stopped from solving these problems because:
* They lack a clear understanding of AI costs and pricing models
* They are unsure about the ROI of AI investments
* They are overwhelmed by the proliferation of low-quality internal tools
* They are hesitant to adopt new AI tools due to high costs
* They are unsure about how to measure the effectiveness of AI services

## Biggest Opportunity
The highest-signal opportunity is to create an **AI Cost Calculator** that helps businesses understand the true cost of adopting AI tools. This is a frequent and severe pain point, with many users expressing frustration with the opaque pricing models of AI providers. A simple and user-friendly cost calculator could help businesses make informed decisions about their AI investments, and willingness to pay for such a tool is estimated to be around $20-$50 per month. As one user succinctly put it, "AI will likely cost $60-$80 per user per month. Akin to an average cellphone bill."
