## Patterns & Triggers
People are trying to:
* Automate manual workflows
* Find and validate startup ideas
* Market and outreach their products
* Automate customer support
* Solve data automation and Excel hell
* Use AI and automation for solo founders
Triggers include:
* Wasting hours on Reddit looking for startup ideas
* Struggling with messy spreadsheets
* Hating marketing and outreach
* Needing to automate customer support
* Wanting to use AI and automation for solo founders

## Objections & Blockers
People are stopped by:
* Lack of simple automation tools
* Difficulty in finding startup ideas
* Limited marketing and outreach skills
* Limited resources and budget
* Limited technical expertise
* Fear of using AI and automation
Solutions that failed include:
* Using existing automation tools that are too complex
* Trying to market and outreach themselves
* Using manual workflows that are time-consuming

## Biggest Opportunity
The biggest opportunity is to create an **Automation Platform for Solo Founders** that solves the problem of "Solutions to build and manage automation easily?" This pain point is frequent, severe, and least served by existing tools. As one user said, "Solutions to build and manage automation easily? Indie hacking is already lonely, now I feel like a part time sysadmin too." This platform could provide a simple and easy-to-use automation tool that helps solo founders and indie hackers automate their workflows, freeing up time for more important tasks.
