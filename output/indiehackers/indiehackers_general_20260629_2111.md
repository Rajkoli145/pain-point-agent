## Patterns & Triggers
* Many indie hackers struggle with validating their product ideas before building them.
* They often spend too much time on ideas that don't work out, leading to frustration and burnout.
* Customer support and communication can be a significant challenge for indie hackers.
* The culture and community of indie hackers can be overwhelming and frustrating.
* Indie hackers may struggle with creating and prioritizing a product roadmap.
* They may feel pressure to constantly launch new products, rather than focusing on creating value.

## Objections & Blockers
* Many indie hackers are unsure of how to validate their product ideas effectively.
* They may lack the resources or expertise to create a successful product.
* The culture and community of indie hackers can be intimidating and overwhelming.
* Indie hackers may feel like they need to constantly launch new products to be successful.
* They may be unsure of how to prioritize their product roadmap and focus on the most important features.

The biggest opportunity is to create a tool that helps indie hackers validate their product ideas before building them.
