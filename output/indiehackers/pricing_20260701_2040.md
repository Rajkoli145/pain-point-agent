## Patterns & Triggers
The patterns and triggers that cause these complaints include:
* Pre-launch anxiety about setting prices
* High costs of hosting and deploying projects
* Difficulty in determining fair pricing
* Uncertainty about pricing models and strategies
* Frustration with hiding pricing and its impact on sales
* Concerns about calculating pricing for lifetime deals and subscription-based models
* Questions about how to set pricing and what pricing models to use

## Objections & Blockers
The objections and blockers that prevent people from solving these problems themselves include:
* Lack of experience and knowledge about pricing strategies
* Fear of setting prices too high or too low
* Uncertainty about the value of their product or service
* Difficulty in calculating costs and determining pricing
* Fear of losing customers due to high prices
* Limited resources and budget to spend on marketing and promotion

## Biggest Opportunity
The single highest-signal opportunity is a **Pricing Strategy Tool** that helps indie hackers and founders determine fair and effective pricing for their products or services. This tool would address the frequent pain point of pricing strategy and provide a solution to the frustration and uncertainty that many indie hackers and founders experience when setting prices. As one user stated, "I'm pre-launch and genuinely stuck on this one. I keep opening the pricing page, typing a number, deleting it, and closing the tab." A pricing strategy tool would provide a clear and structured approach to determining prices, taking into account factors such as costs, competition, and target audience.
