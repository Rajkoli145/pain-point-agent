## Patterns & Triggers
People are struggling with marketing when they:
* Launch a new product
* Try to grow their user base
* Balance development and marketing
* Lack knowledge about marketing
* Want to focus on development instead of marketing
* Try to make money online without relying on marketing

Triggers include:
* Launching a product with no audience
* Spending too much time on marketing
* Feeling overwhelmed by marketing tasks
* Wanting to focus on development instead of marketing
* Trying to make money online without marketing expertise

## Objections & Blockers
People are stopped from solving these problems because:
* They lack knowledge about marketing
* They don't have enough time to focus on marketing
* They are overwhelmed by the number of marketing tools and strategies
* They don't have a big enough budget for marketing
* They are afraid of failing at marketing
