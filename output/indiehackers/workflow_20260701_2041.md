## Patterns & Triggers
People are trying to:
* Automate manual workflows
* Find and validate new ideas
* Source and curate content
* Manage Excel and spreadsheets
* Integrate AI workflows
* Manage time and consistency
Triggers include:
* Feeling overwhelmed by manual tasks
* Struggling to find new ideas
* Wasting time on content curation
* Dealing with Excel and spreadsheet issues
* Wanting to leverage AI workflows
* Needing to improve time management and consistency

## Objections & Blockers
People are stopped by:
* Lack of technical expertise
* Limited resources (time, money)
* Uncertainty about which tools to use
* Fear of wasting time and money on ineffective solutions
* Difficulty in finding the right workflow automation tools
* Previous failed attempts at solving the problem

## Biggest Opportunity
The biggest opportunity is to create a **Workflow Automation Tool** that solves the problem of "I got tired of re-explaining my workflows to AI every single day". This pain point is frequently mentioned (12 posts/comments) and has a high severity level. The tool should be able to automate manual workflows, integrate with AI workflows, and provide a user-friendly interface for solo founders and small businesses. The willingness to pay for such a tool is medium ($20-$50/month), and the difficulty level is medium. As one user mentioned, "I got tired of re-explaining my workflows to AI every single day", highlighting the need for a tool that can remember and automate workflows.
