## Patterns & Triggers
People are frustrated with the lack of effective discovery and promotion tools when trying to launch and grow their products. They are also struggling to manage non-code aspects of their products, such as customer support and sales. Many are wasting hours on manual tasks, such as distribution and data analysis, and are looking for automated tools to simplify these processes. Additionally, indie hackers are seeking curated directories of tools and resources to help them find the best solutions for their needs.

## Objections & Blockers
Many indie hackers are hesitant to invest in new tools and solutions due to limited budgets and uncertainty about the effectiveness of these tools. Others have tried existing solutions and found them to be inadequate or too expensive. Some are also skeptical about the value of automation and are unsure about how to integrate automated tools into their workflows.
