## Patterns & Triggers
People are trying to use Quora for business marketing, but are finding it difficult to maintain and measure the effectiveness of their efforts. They are also looking for ways to automate their business processes and find the right software for managing their business. Additionally, they are frustrated with the limitations of existing business software and are looking for custom solutions. The trigger for these complaints is often the realization that their current tools and processes are not effective or efficient.

## Objections & Blockers
People are stopped from solving these problems themselves by a lack of knowledge or expertise in using Quora for business marketing, automating their business processes, and finding the right software for their needs. They have tried using existing tools and processes, but have found them to be limited or ineffective. They are also blocked by the lack of a comprehensive guide to Quora advertising and the difficulty in measuring the effectiveness of Quora ads.

The single highest-signal opportunity is to create a Quora marketing tool that helps businesses and marketers to effectively use Quora for their marketing efforts.
