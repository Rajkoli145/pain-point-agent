## Patterns & Triggers
People are struggling to find the right productivity tools, manage their tasks, and launch on Product Hunt. They are trying to streamline their workflow, reduce noise, and increase productivity. They have tried various tools and solutions, but are still facing difficulties.

## Objections & Blockers
People are hesitant to try new productivity tools because they are overwhelmed by the amount of options available. They are also struggling to launch on Product Hunt because they don't know how to get featured or get enough upvotes.

## Biggest Opportunity
The biggest opportunity is to create a productivity tool that helps people manage their tasks and time more efficiently. As one user said, "I am not able to manage/fit everything that I wanted to do in my schedule". A tool that solves this problem could be a huge hit, with a willingness to pay signal of $10-20/month. This is because people are struggling to find a tool that meets their needs, and are willing to pay for a solution that can help them streamline their workflow and increase productivity.
